Body Freedom Lead Gen

How This Works

This tool finds Instagram users who are publicly talking about body pain, movement restriction, or somatic struggle — and turns them into warm outreach leads. Follow the pipeline in order: Discover → Queue → Scrape → Score → Leads. Insights and Strategy are available anytime once you have scored data.

1
🔍 Discover Posts

Enter keywords that describe your ideal client's pain — things like chronic pain, tight hips, body tension, nervous system, somatic. Virlo searches Instagram and returns posts where people are actively discussing these topics. These posts are your lead sources.

Tips
  • Prioritize posts with high comment counts — more comments = more potential leads
  • Try 3–5 specific keywords per search for the best signal-to-noise ratio
  • "This month" is a good default time range to start
2
📋 Queue

Review the discovered posts and choose which ones are worth scraping. Not every post is a good lead source — you're looking for posts with authentic, personal engagement, not just viral hype content.

Tips
  • Aim for 5–15 posts per batch to keep scrape time and cost manageable
  • Look for posts where comments feel personal and vulnerable, not just "great post!"
  • Avoid queuing posts you've already scraped — it creates duplicate leads
3
⚡ Scrape

Runs an automated scraper (via Apify) across all queued posts and pulls down every comment — username, comment text, and timestamp. This runs in the background; the progress bar updates live. A typical batch of 10 posts takes 2–5 minutes.

Tips
  • Enable "Fetch reply threads" to detect whether the creator already replied to a commenter (adds scrape time)
  • Max 500 comments scraped per post
  • You can navigate away — the scrape continues in the background
4
🎯 Score

Claude reads every scraped comment and scores it 1–3 based on how clearly the person expresses personal pain or struggle. It also generates a personalized DM draft for warm and hot leads.

Score key
  • ● 3 — Hot — Direct personal struggle (body tension, pain, disconnection, movement avoidance)
  • ● 2 — Warm — Mild resonance or curiosity, no clear personal pain expressed
  • ● 1 — Cold — Generic, emoji-only, spam, or unrelated
5
👥 Leads

Browse all scored leads, filter by score or status, and manage your outreach. Edit Claude's DM drafts before sending, then update each lead's status as you make contact.

Tips
  • Status flow: New → Contacted → Responded → Not Relevant
  • Start with Score 3 (Hot) leads — they're the most likely to convert
6
💡 Insights

Once you have 30–50 scored leads, run a market intelligence analysis. Claude synthesizes all the comments to identify exact language, recurring pain points, emotional signals, and content hooks your audience uses.

Tips
  • Run after each new scrape batch to keep insights fresh
  • Use "Specific orbit" scope to analyze one competitor's audience in isolation
7
🗺️ Strategy

Generated automatically after an Insights run. Contains fully-formed content pillars, video concepts, hooks, and a posting strategy tailored to the exact language and pain points your audience uses.
